    Welcome to Sheraton Can Tho

    Five-star hotel accommodations await in Can Tho

    Regarded as a gem in the Western Capital, Sheraton Can Tho is a five-star hotel in the heart of Can Tho, Vietnam. Adjacent to the Ninh Kieu Pier and settled in the Mekong River Delta, our hotel boasts sweeping views of Can Tho City and the Hau River. Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Vietnam when you relax in our hotel's well-shaded garden or listen to the water echoing along the Mekong River. Our prime location places you moments from popular attractions like the Can Tho Bridge, the Binh Thuy Temple and the Cai Rang Floating Market. Unwind in beautifully appointed rooms, complemented by five-star hotel amenities, including an on-site pampering spa and an outdoor pool. Whether you are craving breakfast or dinner in Can Tho, we offer an array of hotel dining, such as a Southern Vietnamese restaurant and a sunset lounge. Host corporate and social events in Can Tho City, courtesy of our hotel's Convention Center featuring two grand ballrooms and four sophisticated meeting rooms.
